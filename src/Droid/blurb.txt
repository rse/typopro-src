Droid is a font family first released in 2007 and created by Ascender
Corporation for use by the Open Handset Alliance platform Android. The
fonts are intended for use on the small screens of mobile handsets and
were designed by Steve Matteson of Ascender Corporation. The name was
derived from the Open Handset Alliance platform name Android.
