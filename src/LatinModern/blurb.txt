Derivation: Computer Modern

The Latin Modern fonts are derived from the famous Computer Modern fonts
designed by Donald E. Knuth and described in Volume E of his Computers
& Typesetting series. For TypoPRO the font set were reduced to the 10pt
variants and named after their long font name.
