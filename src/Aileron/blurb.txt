Designed:   2013
Derivation: Helvetica

Aileron is a high quality sans serif font designed by Sora Sagano. With
the reference from the popular font Helvetica, Sora Sagano added his own
touch to the design to make it more curvy and elegant. According to him,
the design was close to Helvetica, but closer to Univers in terms of
concept.
