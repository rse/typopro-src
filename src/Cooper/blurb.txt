Cooper* is a meticulously researched, historically accurate revival of
the Cooper series designed by Oswald Cooper and released by Barnhart
Brothers & Spindler in the 1920s. Cooper* is designed and maintained by
Owen Earl, who is the creator of the font foundry indestructible type*.
