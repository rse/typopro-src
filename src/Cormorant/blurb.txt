Cormorant is a free display type family developed by Christian Thalmann
(Catharsis Fonts). It comprises a total of 45 font files spanning 9
different styles (Roman, Italic, Infant, Infant Italic, Garamond,
Garamond Italic, Upright Cursive, Small Caps, Unicase) and 5 weights
(Light, Regular, Medium, Semibold, Bold).
