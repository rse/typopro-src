Libertinus fonts is a fork of the Linux Libertine and Linux Biolinum
fonts that started as an OpenType math companion of the Libertine font
family. It has grown to a full fork to address some of the bugs in the
fonts. Thanks to Frédéric Wang for coming up with the name Libertinus.
